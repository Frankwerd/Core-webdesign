Our History & Team

With the desire to exemplify our disciplines in our hearts, we provide over CORESSWC. We started October of 2014 as a social group seeking spiritual and wellness connection. Determined to lead a purpose-driven life, we formed a Not For Profit Organization in June 2016.

We are a group of multicultural women who are determined to seek our meaning, value, and life purpose. We are a new community service organization, and our own experiences are key drivers for the initial causes we chose. We are determined to make a positive difference in the lives that we touch.

Our Vision

Serve the community by supporting abused women and complimenting school's financial literacy curriculum for teens.

Our Mission

Connecting with one abused woman and one teen at a time to adapt strong, measurable CORE values and disciplines.
