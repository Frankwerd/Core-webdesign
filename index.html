Empowering Communities Through Financial Wellness

CORESSWC is dedicated to providing financial literacy education and support to individuals and families, fostering economic stability and growth.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

As a 501(c)3 non-profit organization, we serve the community by supporting abused women and complimenting school's financial literacy curriculum for teens.

Our Mission

Connect with abused women; youths to adapt strong financial literacy disciplines; applying measurable CORE values and disciplines for all programs. As a measure of success share proven best practices and compare adaptation rates year on year.

CORE Programs

Abused Women Support

Free of violence and abuse against Women in their homes, workplace and in their communities

Adopt-a-DAP

Support for families in need through our Adopt-a-DAP program.

Financial Discipline Award (FDA)

Compliment State Mandated Financial Literacy for students (1st graders - high school seniors)

Financial Readiness Academic Scholarship Award (FRASA)

Work with high school educators to promote proficiency in financial readiness as they exit high school.

Financial Literacy Coaching for Adults

Financial literacy programs for adults seeking the necessary tools to enjoy financial freedom.
