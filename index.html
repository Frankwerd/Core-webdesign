Empowering Communities Through Financial Wellness

CORESSWC is dedicated to providing financial literacy education and support to individuals and families, fostering economic stability and growth.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals and families to achieve financial independence and build a secure future through comprehensive financial education and support services.

Financial Literacy

Comprehensive financial education workshops and resources.

Adopt-a-DAP

Support for families in need through our Adopt-a-DAP program.

FDA Program

Financial Development Assistance for small businesses and entrepreneurs.

Financial Aid Readiness

Scholarship program to help students prepare for financial aid.

Adult Literacy

Literacy programs for adults seeking to improve their reading and writing skills.
